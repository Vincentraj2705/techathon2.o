Frequently Asked Questions

Can I participate individually?

All teams must consist of exactly 4 members. This requirement is mandatory for participation.

When is the registration deadline?

Registration closes on January 22, 2026. Make sure to register before this date!

Do I need to bring my own laptop?

Yes, participants must bring their own laptops and necessary equipment for development.

Can I change my problem statement after registration?

Problem statement changes may be allowed before January 20, 2026. Contact us for assistance.

Will I receive a certificate?

Yes, all participants will receive certificates of participation. Winners will receive special certificates of achievement.
