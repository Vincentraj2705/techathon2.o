About TECHATHON 2K26

Nova Nexus Hub

Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering

"Where Innovation Meets Excellence - Empowering Tomorrow's Tech Leaders"

Nova Nexus Hub is the premier innovation and technology center of the ECE Department at Kings Engineering College. We are committed to fostering creativity, technical excellence, and problem-solving skills among students. Through TECHATHON 2K26, we aim to provide a platform for brilliant minds to showcase their talents and develop innovative solutions to real-world challenges.

Innovation Challenge

TECHATHON 2K26 is an intensive innovation challenge where participants compete to solve real-world problems using cutting-edge technology. This internal hackathon brings together the brightest minds from our college.

Collaborative Learning

Work as a Team. Collaborate, brainstorm, and build innovative solutions. Network with fellow students and learn from each other's expertise.

Recognition & Growth

Top 3 teams will receive exciting prizes and recognition. All participants will receive certificates of participation. Gain valuable experience and showcase your skills to faculty and industry.

42 Problem Statements

Choose from 42 diverse problem statements spanning Environment Management, Smart Automation, Medical Technology, Agriculture, Education, Renewable Energy, and more.

Learn & Build

Access mentorship, resources, and guidance throughout the event. Learn new technologies, improve your problem-solving skills, and add impressive projects to your portfolio.
